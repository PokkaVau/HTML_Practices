Ingredients

  • 1 cup(2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F(175°C)
  2. In a large bowl, cream together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar
  3. Beat the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla
  4. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a separate bowl
  5. Gradually blend the dry ingredients into the wet mixture
  6. Stir in the chocolate chip
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto ungreased baking sheets
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until edges are golden brown
  9. Cool on wire racks and enjoy!

Notes

  • You can refrigerate the dough for chewier cookies
  • Add chopped nuts for extra crunch
